Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How far in advance should I order?
We recommend ordering at least 5-7 days in advance for standard custom cakes. For wedding cakes or elaborate designs, please contact us 2-4 weeks ahead to ensure we can bring your vision to life.
Do you offer tastings?
Yes! We offer complimentary tastings for wedding cakes and orders over $150. Schedule your tasting appointment by calling us or sending a message through our contact form.
Can I customize any design?
Absolutely! Our online cake builder covers most customization options. If you have a special request or unique design in mind, contact us and we'll work with you to create the perfect cake.
Do you deliver?
We offer local delivery within 15 miles for a flat fee of $15. For weddings and large events, we provide setup and delivery services - contact us for a custom quote based on your needs.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and cash. A 50% deposit is required to confirm custom cake orders, with the balance due upon pickup.
